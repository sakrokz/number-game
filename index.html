How to Play

Objective:

The goal of the game is to match the target number by using the provided numbers and basic arithmetic operators.

Instructions:



Target Number:

Each level begins with a randomly generated target number.

Number Buttons:

Click on the number buttons to build an expression.

The expression is formed by combining numbers and operators.

Operator Buttons:

Click on the operator buttons to perform operations in your expression.

Clear Button:

Click the "Clear" button to reset the work area and start a new expression.

Next Level:

Click "Next Level" to advance to the next level with an increased difficulty.

Scoring:

Each level successfully completed adds points to your score.

Good luck and have fun playing the game!
